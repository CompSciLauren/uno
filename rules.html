Rule and Information:

  • This is a modified game of Uno, matching color or card value.
  • The draw-4 cards can be stacked. This means if one player pays a draw-4, the other player can also play a draw-4 in order to force the next player to either play a draw-4 card or draw the total number of cards from the draw-4 cards already played. This same rule applies to the draw-2 card.
  • You must call uno BEFORE playing your second-to-last card. Failing to do so will result in a card penalty.
  • If you do not have a valid card to play you must draw until you get one you can play.
