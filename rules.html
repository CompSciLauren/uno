Rule and Information:

  • This is a modified game of Uno, matching color or card value.
  • Card colors can be Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow. Wildcard colors can choose from all 4.
  • Card values can be 0-9, draw 2, switch directions, and skips for each color. Wildcards can be color choice or additionally draw 4.
  • To play a card simply click on the card you wish to play.
  • You must call uno BEFORE playing your second-to-last card. Failing to do so will result in a card penalty.
  • If you do not have a valid card to play you must draw until you get one you can play.
  • There are no special rules in this version (e.g swap decks).
  • Bots play by the same rules as you do.
  • Bots require some time to "think".
