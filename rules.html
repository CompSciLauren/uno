Rule and Information:

  • This is a modified game of Uno, matching color or card value
  • Cards with value 10 are Draw 2
  • Cards with value 11 are Reverse Play Order
  • Cards with value 12 are Skip next Player's Turn
  • Cards can be Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow. 0-9, +2, switch directions, and skips for each color
  • To play a card simply enter the index of the card starting at the leftmost card at 0 (0, 1, 2, 3, ... )
  • You do not have to directly call Uno, to win you simply play your final card
  • If you do not have a valid card to play you must draw until you get one you can play
  • There are no special rules in this version (e.g swap decks)
  • Bots play by the same rules as you do
  • Bots require some time to "think"
